Applied Research: Auditing AI-Generated Hiring Assessments

M.A. Ethics of Artificial Intelligence | University of Guelph | Completed June 2025

This applied research project audited the reliability, alignment, and fairness of ChatGPT-generated interview evaluations compared to human ratings in automated video interview (AVI) settings—a technology increasingly deployed by organizations for candidate screening.

Methodology: Using a sample of 183 transcribed candidate responses, I assessed how closely AI-generated scores matched those of trained human evaluators, and whether this alignment varied across gender, race, and age subgroups using statistical analysis (ICC, Pearson correlation, paired t-tests, Fisher's r-to-z transformation).

Key Findings: While results showed strong overall correlation (ICC = 0.94), significant divergences emerged: ChatGPT consistently scored candidates higher (+6.5 points on average) and exhibited concerning bias patterns—particularly underrating Black participants and overrating middle-aged candidates. These discrepancies were not present in human ratings.

Business Implications: This research demonstrates that AI evaluation tools, while promising for efficiency, require rigorous fairness audits and human oversight before deployment. Organizations using or considering AI hiring tools must implement validation frameworks to ensure compliance with equity standards and avoid discriminatory outcomes.

Undergraduate Honours Thesis

B.A. Psychology (Co-op Honours) | University of Guelph | 2024

"Exploring Ethical Considerations: Generative AI's Impact on Current & Future HR Practices"

This research project explores the ethical implications of Generative AI adoption in Human Resources settings. Through qualitative analysis and literature review, I examined how GAI tools affect day-to-day HR tasks, from recruitment and onboarding to performance management and employee relations.

The study investigates questions of fairness, transparency, accountability, and human agency in AI-augmented HR workflows, offering recommendations for responsible implementation and oversight.
